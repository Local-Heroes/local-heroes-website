Program Highlights

At Local Heroes, we've worked hard to develop a variety of courses and programs to fit everyone's needs. Whether you want to improve existing skills or expand your horizons, you've come to the right place. Check out our website to see what we have to offer, and learn at your own pace anytime, anywhere!

Guided Mentoring

Students will be guided about various concepts of STEM via the Local Heroes' mentors.

Hands-on Projects

Students will be taught various concepts of STEM using hands-on projects.

Preperation for College

Step-by-step instructions and to-do tasks for students on how to plan early for their college applications.

Resources for Students

Students will be taught subjects through guided hands-on projects, and will be mentored on enhancing their portfolios for college applications.

Resources for Teachers

Teachers have access to books based on various subjects via the Offline Library for their reference.

Offline Library
